5. Product Scope

DIDI.BIKE products and services are strictly limited to the following domains:

  • Bicycle Telemetry Sensor Hardware — Precision sensors for measuring aerodynamic drag, power, cadence, speed, and environmental conditions
  • Aerodynamic Analysis Systems — Real-time CdA (coefficient of drag area) measurement and wind tunnel correlation tools
  • Biomechanical Data Acquisition — Motion capture and pedaling dynamics analysis for bike fitting and performance optimization
  • Cycling Performance Analytics — Software platforms and dashboards for visualizing and interpreting sensor data
  • OEM Sensor Integration — Custom sensor modules and APIs for integration with third-party cycling computers, smart trainers, and coaching platforms
