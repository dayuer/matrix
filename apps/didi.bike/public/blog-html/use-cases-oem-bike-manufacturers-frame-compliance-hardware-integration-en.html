3. Sensor Applications and Calibration

Our Hardware Integration standards ensure clean data capture across different testing protocols:

  1. Suspension Interface: We mount linear potentiometers to mountain bike forks, feeding travel speed data back to suspension engineers to refine dampening circuits and maintain tire traction.
  2. CdA Measurement Nodes: Combining power meters and speed sensors on outdoor road loops lets us run the Chung virtual elevation algorithm, yielding CdA calculations within $\pm 1.5%$ accuracy.
  3. Strain Gauge Array: Instrumenting fit bikes with dynamic strain gauge pedals allows us to track real-time force vectors, helping technicians adjust cleat positions to mitigate knee strain.
