1. System Integration & Open Data Flow

When you are deep in the red zone, tasting blood, and holding the wheel in front of you, every single watt matters. Using custom scripting lets us integrate ride data directly into our telemetry dashboards:

  1. Garmin Connect IQ SDK Injection: Seeing dynamic CdA and tire pressure changes live on my screen helps me adjust my position on the saddle to find free speed.
