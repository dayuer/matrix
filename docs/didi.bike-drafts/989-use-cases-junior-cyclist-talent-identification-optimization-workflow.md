# Understanding Junior Cyclist Talent Identification through Optimization Workflow

## 1. Case Study & Engineering Application
Scouting systems are only as good as the decisions they enable. In junior cyclist talent identification, our product strategy centers on building an optimization workflow that simplifies raw data collection and provides instant feedback for young riders. We design software tools that help development squads structure their tests, allowing coaches to run standardized assessments that highlight aerodynamic efficiency, physical dampening capacity, and joint health without needing a PhD in sports science.

For premium structures such as Tudor Pro Cycling, our platform optimizes the entire scouting pipeline. The software maps rider metrics, showing directors a unified ranking of junior talent based on standardized wind telemetry and power profiles.

## 2. Mechanical Power and Vibration Physics
To assess how smoothly an athlete handles rough roads, our system processes accelerometer data to calculate mechanical stress and dampening using standard equations:

$$ a_{\text{vibration}} = \sqrt{\frac{1}{T} \int_0^T [a(t)]^2 \, dt} $$

Where:
*   $P_{\text{total}}$ represents the total mechanical power output required to overcome gravity, aerodynamic drag, rolling resistance, and drivetrain friction.
*   $a_{\text{vibration}}$ is the root-mean-square acceleration of frame vibration, measured via triaxial accelerometers to evaluate dampening characteristics.
*   $\eta_{\text{drafting}}$ represents the drafting efficiency coefficient, representing the percentage reduction in CdA when riding in a slipstream.

## 3. Practical Field Implementations & Optimization Workflow
We design our optimization workflow to resolve scouting challenges through structured, repeatable steps:
1.  **Suspension Telemetry Validation**: The app aggregates fork travel data from linear potentiometers, giving developmental riders clear visual targets to improve their descending skills and traction control.
2.  **Chung Virtual Elevation Field Protocols**: Our guided wizard prompts athletes through steady road laps, calculating CdA changes with $\pm 1.5\%$ precision to identify aerodynamic potential.
3.  **Pedal Stroke Optimization**: The interface displays dynamic force vectors, providing fitters with recommendations to adjust cleat alignment and correct biomechanical issues early in a rider's career.
