FAQ

Do I need internet to use VoiceBridge?
No. VoiceBridge uses the SenseVoice on-device AI engine. Core recording and transcription features work completely offline, even in Airplane Mode.
What languages are supported?
Offline support for Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, Cantonese, French, German, and Spanish. Additional languages (Indonesian, Thai, Vietnamese, Malay) are available through Apple's iOS Speech engine.
Is my recording data safe?
Absolutely. All recordings and transcripts are stored only on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server, and we collect no personal information. See our Privacy Policy.
Is VoiceBridge really completely free? Are there any ads or limits?
Yes, VoiceBridge is completely free. We have no in-app purchases, no ads, and all features (including speaker voiceprint library, local auto-backup, PDF export, etc.) are fully unlocked without any limits on duration or meeting count.
Will deleting the App delete my meeting transcripts?
Yes. Since VoiceBridge runs 100% offline, all recordings and transcripts are saved only in your iPhone's local secure sandbox. Uninstalling the app will clear all local data. Please make sure to export your important files as PDF/TXT before uninstalling.
